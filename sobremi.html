Sobre mi

My name is Julian, I'm 29 years old and I live in Bogota.
My life is almost all about two things: Physical training and creativity

Physical training because I was bullied by my father when I was a child (fondly fortunately)
so I told my mom and dad that I wanted to start working out in a gym at 14 years old.
Later on I decided to study Cultura Fisica y Deporte
So I have almost 15 years of physical training over my back, I practice olympic weighlifting,
bodybuilding, running, basic gimnastics and calisthenics, and currently training/learning some of muay thay
besides the physical aspect, physical training makes me really happy,
and taught me the real meaning of discipline and what you can get with it

Creativity because I love to create my own stuff.
I studied some graphic design as well so I got some base for creativity in those years.
My mom is a tailor so I learned some of her craft, now I am creating my own bussiness
where I design, sew and sell my products, functional stuff like bags, backpacks, chest bags, phone holders etc.

Currently learning some of 3d design, german, muay thay, and programming. I'd like to create robots in the future
